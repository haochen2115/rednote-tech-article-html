visual reference for tech-paper-card

card with all four label colors
01
Red Label

Card with red label

Red is for phenomena, limits, and opening cards. Use for first card in a sequence or for failure modes.

02
Green Label

Card with green label

Green is for mechanisms, working solutions, and resolutions. Use for cards explaining how something works.

03
Blue Label

Card with blue label

Blue is for technical details, measurements, and citations. Use for evidence-heavy cards.

04
Gold Label

Card with gold label

Gold is for synthesis and deep observations. Use sparingly — no more than one per post.

Insight variants
